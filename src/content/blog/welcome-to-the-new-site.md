We're excited to launch the redesigned WP Product Talk website. Built with modern web technologies, this new site is faster, more accessible, and gives us a proper home for written content alongside the podcast.

## What's New

- **Fresh design** — Dark editorial aesthetic that puts content front and center
- **Real-time episodes** — Episodes pulled directly from our RSS feed, always up to date
- **This blog** — A place for longer-form articles, show notes, and insights from the WP product community
- **Faster performance** — Static site generation means near-instant page loads

## What's Staying the Same

The podcast itself isn't going anywhere. New episodes every week, same great hosts, same honest conversations about building WordPress products.

You can still find us on [Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/wp-product-talk/id1716538393), [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/5Z8P6FcCekJFAAHAKEl2i3), and everywhere else you listen.

## What's Next

We'll be using this blog to share episode summaries, deep-dive articles on topics from the show, and guest posts from members of the WordPress product community. If you've got something to share, [reach out to Matt](mailto:matt@wpproducttalk.com).

Stay tuned.
